About Me

I am Dmitriy. All my friends call me Dima.

My first step in IT sphere was the course Way up. From the very beginning I understood that web development is not just about HTML and CSS. Right after that course I started learning JavaScript at Udemy and practicing in RS School. My persistence let me study hard despite the fact that I had a full-time job. But it was really exhausting to meet the deadlines at the IT course and to work at the same time. But I am convinced that thanks to my previous position (I was an officer in 56 signal regiment) I developed excellent analysis and problem solving skills and that helped me to start the course Redev Education. At this course I enriched my JavaScript knowledge and I am sure that was because of good structured information, thought-out deadlines in the form of check-lists to be fulfilled and tracking of problems to be solved. At the same time I am upgrading my kyu at Codewars.

At the moment my technical stack is stuffed with: HTML, CSS, SASS (in progress), Bootstrap, JS, TypeScript (in progress), React/Redux/React native(in progress), Git.

My friends describe me as a reliable and responsive person. My wife thinks that I have got golden hands and a head full of challenging ideas. My children consider me to be a magician always ready to fix anything up and fulfill their wish.

My goal is to become a full stack developer where I can realize all my ideas and upgrade my knowledge. At the same time I want to learn new technologies in order to use them in diverse projects. One more desire I have is to share my knowledge, skills and experience with other people.
